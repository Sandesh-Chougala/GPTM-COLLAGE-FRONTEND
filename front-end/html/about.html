History

Government Polytechnic Mudhol established in the year 2009 , is one among 81 government institutions under DTE Karnataka. The institution is offering four courses namely Civil Engineering, Computer Science and Engineering ,Electronics and Communications Engineering and Mechanical Engineering.The institute has 31 teaching staff and 6 supporting staff.
