Message from the Head of Department(CE)

Welcome to the Department of Civil Engineering!

As the backbone of infrastructure and sustainable development, Civil Engineering plays a vital role in shaping the world around us. At CE, we are dedicated to cultivating engineers who are not only technically proficient but also socially responsible and environmentally conscious.

Our curriculum blends core engineering principles with hands-on experience in areas such as Structural Engineering, Geotechnical Engineering, Transportation Systems, Environmental Engineering, and Construction Management. We emphasize innovation, safety, and sustainability in every aspect of learning.

Students are encouraged to participate in fieldwork, internships, and collaborative projects that bridge academia and industry. Our department fosters a culture of inquiry, leadership, and community engagement.

We take pride in our vibrant network of students, faculty, and alumni who contribute to building resilient and future-ready infrastructure. Whether you're a prospective student, a parent, or a visitor, I invite you to explore the opportunities and impact that await you at CE.
