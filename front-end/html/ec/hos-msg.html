Message from the Head of Department(E&C)

Welcome to the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering!

In an era where connectivity and intelligent systems shape the way we live and work, our department is committed to nurturing the next generation of engineers and innovators. At E&C, we believe that engineering education goes beyond circuits and signals—it's about fostering analytical thinking, creativity, and a passion for solving real-world problems.

Our curriculum integrates strong theoretical foundations with practical exposure to technologies such as Embedded Systems, VLSI Design, Wireless Communication, Signal Processing, and the Internet of Things (IoT). We encourage students to engage in research, participate in technical events, and collaborate with industry to gain holistic learning experiences.

We are proud of our dynamic community of students, faculty, and alumni who continue to make impactful contributions across various domains. Whether you're a prospective student, a parent, or a visitor, I invite you to discover the exciting possibilities that await you at E&C.

Together, let’s engineer a smarter and more connected world.

Warm regards
Mr. Sanjeev Kullur
