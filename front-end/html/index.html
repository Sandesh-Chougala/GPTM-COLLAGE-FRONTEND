Vision and Mision

Vision

To impart technical knowledge, professional skills, moral values, enabling a student to be globally competent in the field of engineering and to serve the society to make the world a better place to live in

Mission

  • To provide an academic environment, through competitive curriculum in collaboration with industry, conducive for high quality education focusing on transfer of knowledge and skill + development for the benefit of the profession and the society.
  • To nurture talent in students to enable them to be leaders in their chosen professions while maintaining the highest level of ethics.
  • To promote the spirit of enquiry, innovation, life skills and to encourage entrepreneurship.
  • To foster effective interactions and networking with all the stakeholders so as to work towards the growth and sustainability of the society and environment.

Principal Message

It is my immense pleasure to invite the aspiring students who are keen to make a best career in technical field with an aim to become techno-supervisor in manufacturing area of diverse range of industrial products.

The Diploma holders play very important roles in industrial and economic developments of the country. They acquire specialized skill in the chosen areas and provide much needed supports in any engineering establishments. Besides, the Students with Diploma in Engineering also provide support services in various other sectors of economy including service sector.

It is well established that our country needs large number of skilled manpower to keep pace of current and future requirements. The Diploma holders are most sought after these days and they are quickly employed in any industry.

We, at Government Polytechnic, MUDHOL, take every care to meet the level of aspirations of all students by providing best physical and academic infrastructure needed to make them competent technical professionals. Our quality faculty adds value to our best endeavor in providing technical trainings to our students in the interest of all the stake holders in the country.

I welcome you all to join our institute and make a best career in life.
