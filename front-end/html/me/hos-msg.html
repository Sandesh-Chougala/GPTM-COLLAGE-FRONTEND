Message from the Head of Department(ME)

Welcome to the Department of Mechanical Engineering!

Mechanical Engineering is the backbone of industrial innovation and technological advancement. From designing sustainable energy systems to developing autonomous vehicles and advanced robotics, our discipline continues to shape the world around us.

At our department, we foster a culture of excellence, creativity, and collaboration. Our curriculum is carefully crafted to provide a strong foundation in core mechanical principles while integrating emerging technologies such as Mechatronics, Additive Manufacturing, and Computational Fluid Dynamics. Through hands-on labs, industry projects, and research initiatives, we prepare our students to tackle real-world challenges with confidence and ingenuity.

We are proud of our dedicated faculty, passionate students, and accomplished alumni who exemplify the spirit of engineering leadership. Whether you're here to learn, collaborate, or innovate, we welcome you to be part of our journey toward engineering a better tomorrow.

With warm regards,
Mr Mahadev Katti
